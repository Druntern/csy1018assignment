Software Engineering

In this video, a software engineer vaguely explains how is her work. Software engineers don’t usually work alone, most of the times they collaborate with other people like designers. In her case, she normally spends most of her day coding and debugging, and has some reunions with the team so they can talk about what they are doing and future ideas.
